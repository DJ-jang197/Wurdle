How to play

  1. Guess the 5-letter secret word in 8 tries. Every guess must be a valid English dictionary word.
  2. Grid tile colors
    • Orange. Right letter, right place.
    • Yellow. In the word, wrong place for that guess.
    • Pale gold. Not in the word for that guess.
  3. Orange locks a letter. Locked letters appear in the row above the grid and never change again.
  4. After each wrong guess, one unlocked letter in the secret changes to another valid word. A glow on the locked row shows which spot changed.
  5. The secret can change after each wrong guess. Grid tiles always show the result from when that guess was made. On the keyboard, yellow clears if that letter is no longer in the secret after a mutation.
  6. Keyboard colors
    • Orange. Right letter, right place on a past guess. Stays colored.
    • Yellow. In the word at wrong place on a past guess. Stays colored unless the secret mutates that letter out.
    • Neutral. You have no feedback for this letter yet.
    • Amber. You used this letter in your most recent guess.
  7. Row numbers beside the grid mark attempts 1 through 8.
  8. Win by matching the current secret exactly. Your score is your time (lower is better).
