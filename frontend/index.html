How to play

  1. Guess the 5-letter secret word in 8 tries.
  2. Each guess must be a valid English dictionary word.
  3. Tile colors mean:
    • Orange — correct letter, correct spot
    • Yellow — letter is in the word, wrong spot
    • Gray — letter is not in the word
  4. Orange locks a letter. Locked letters appear above and never mutate again.
  5. After each wrong guess, one unlocked letter in the secret changes — the glowing slot in the locked row shows which position changed.
  6. Win by matching the current secret exactly. Your score is your time (lower is better).
